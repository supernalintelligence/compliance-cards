### Professional Certifications

Recommended certifications for compliance professionals:

- **Privacy**: CIPP (Certified Information Privacy Professional), CIPM, CIPT
- **Security**: CISSP, CISM, CISA
- **Quality**: CQA, CQE (ASQ certifications)
- **Compliance**: CCEP, CCEP-I (SCCE certifications)
- **Audit**: CIA (Certified Internal Auditor)
- **Medical Devices**: RAC (Regulatory Affairs Certification)
- **GxP**: Various vendor-specific (e.g., PDA, ISPE)
