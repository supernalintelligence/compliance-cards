### Communication Frequency & Channels

| Stakeholder | Frequency | Primary Channel | Secondary Channel | Response Time Expected |
|------------|-----------|----------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| **External Auditors** | Annual + interim | Email, audit portal | Video calls | 5-10 business days |
| **FDA** | As required | Electronic submissions (eSTAR) | Formal letters | 30-90 days (submissions) |
| **HHS/OCR** | Breach/complaint driven | Online portal | Formal letters | 30 days (complaints) |
| **DPAs** | Breach/complaint driven | Email, online portal | Formal letters | 72 hours (breaches) |
| **Certification Bodies** | Annual | Email | Audit portal | 5-10 business days |
| **Critical Vendors** | Quarterly | Email, vendor portal | Phone, meetings | 1-5 business days |
| **Customers (B2B)** | On request | Trust center, email | Customer portal | 5-10 business days |
| **Insurance** | Annual + incident | Email, claims portal | Phone (urgent) | 24-48 hours (incidents) |
| **Legal Counsel** | As needed | Email, secure messaging | Phone, meetings | 1-3 business days |
