### Month-by-Month External Stakeholder Involvement

This section shows when and how external stakeholders typically engage throughout a compliance project.

#### **Months 0-3: Planning & Setup**

**External Auditors**:
- ✅ Auditor selection and engagement (if not already retained)
- ✅ Initial scoping meetings
- ✅ Preliminary timeline discussion
- **Time Commitment**: 4-8 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: Usually included in audit fee

**Certification Bodies** (ISO):
- ✅ Application for certification
- ✅ Scope definition discussions
- ✅ Pre-assessment (optional but recommended)
- **Time Commitment**: 8-12 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: Application fee ($500-$2,000) + optional pre-assessment ($3,000-$8,000)

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Gap assessment
- ✅ Roadmap development
- ✅ Policy templates
- **Time Commitment**: 40-120 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: $6,000-$48,000

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Regulatory interpretation
- ✅ Initial policy review
- **Time Commitment**: 10-20 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: $3,000-$16,000

**Vendors**:
- ✅ Initial vendor risk assessments
- ✅ Contract negotiations (BAAs, DPAs)
- **Time Commitment**: 20-40 hours (internal team)

**Regulators**:
- ⚠️ Limited interaction (background research, guidance review)
- ⚠️ FDA Pre-Submission meetings (optional, for complex devices)

---

#### **Months 3-6: Policy Development & Implementation Planning**

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Policy development support
- ✅ Control design guidance
- ✅ Training material development
- **Time Commitment**: 60-100 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: $9,000-$40,000

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Policy and procedure review
- ✅ Contract template review (BAAs, DPAs, vendor agreements)
- ✅ Regulatory correspondence (if needed)
- **Time Commitment**: 15-30 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: $4,500-$24,000

**External Auditors**:
- ✅ Optional mid-project check-in (informal)
- **Time Commitment**: 1-2 hours
- **Cost**: Usually pro bono (relationship building)

**Vendors**:
- ✅ Deep-dive security assessments for critical vendors
- ✅ Vendor compliance evidence collection
- **Time Commitment**: 30-60 hours (internal team)

**Certification Bodies**:
- ⚠️ Minimal interaction (Q&A as needed)

**Regulators**:
- ⚠️ None typically (unless premarket submission in progress)

---

#### **Months 6-12: Implementation & Evidence Collection**

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Implementation support (as needed)
- ✅ Control effectiveness review
- **Time Commitment**: 20-60 hours over 6 months
- **Cost**: $3,000-$24,000

**Vendors**:
- ✅ Implementation partner engagement (technical controls)
- ✅ Tool deployment support
- **Time Commitment**: Variable by project
- **Cost**: Variable by scope

**External Auditors**:
- ⚠️ Minimal interaction (unless requesting interim review)

**Certification Bodies**:
- ⚠️ Minimal interaction

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Ad-hoc questions (5-15 hours over 6 months, $1,500-$12,000)

**Regulators**:
- ⚠️ None typically
- ⚠️ FDA: If submitting premarket application, heavy engagement (months 9-18)

---

#### **Months 12-15: Pre-Audit Preparation**

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Readiness assessment
- ✅ Mock audit
- ✅ Evidence review
- **Time Commitment**: 40-80 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: $6,000-$32,000

**External Auditors**:
- ✅ Planning meeting (4-6 weeks before audit)
- ✅ Evidence request list (PBC - Provided By Client)
- ✅ Stage 1 audit (ISO) - document review
- **Time Commitment**: 8-40 hours over 3 months
- **Cost**: Included in audit fee

**Certification Bodies**:
- ✅ Stage 1 audit scheduling
- ✅ Document submission
- **Time Commitment**: 16-24 hours (audit + prep)
- **Cost**: Included in certification fee

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Audit preparation review
- ✅ Response strategies
- **Time Commitment**: 5-10 hours
- **Cost**: $1,500-$8,000

**Vendors**:
- ✅ Vendor compliance evidence updates
- ✅ SOC 2 report collection from vendors

**Customers**:
- ⚠️ May be engaged if customer audits scheduled

---

#### **Months 15-18: External Audit**

**External Auditors** (SOC 2):
- ✅ On-site audit (or remote)
- ✅ Interviews with staff
- ✅ Control testing
- ✅ Daily debriefs
- ✅ Draft report review
- **Time Commitment**: 40-120 hours (internal team support) over 4-6 weeks
- **Cost**: Audit fee ($25,000-$80,000 for SOC 2 Type II)

**Certification Bodies** (ISO):
- ✅ Stage 2 audit (on-site)
- ✅ Opening meeting, closing meeting
- ✅ Non-conformity review
- **Time Commitment**: 16-40+ hours (audit) + 20-40 hours (internal prep/support)
- **Cost**: Certification fee ($8,000-$30,000 for Stage 2)

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Available for questions during audit
- ✅ Review of findings and management responses
- **Time Commitment**: 5-15 hours
- **Cost**: $1,500-$12,000

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Audit support (if engaged)
- ✅ Respond to findings
- **Time Commitment**: 20-40 hours over 4-6 weeks
- **Cost**: $3,000-$16,000

**Regulators**:
- ⚠️ FDA: If premarket submission, may have questions/responses during review period
- ⚠️ HHS/OCR: Only if under investigation or complaint
- ⚠️ DPAs: Only if under investigation

---

#### **Months 18-21: Post-Audit & Remediation**

**External Auditors**:
- ✅ Findings discussion
- ✅ Corrective action plan review
- ✅ Final report issuance
- ✅ Certificate or attestation delivery
- **Time Commitment**: 8-24 hours (internal team)
- **Cost**: Included in audit fee

**Certification Bodies**:
- ✅ Non-conformity remediation evidence review
- ✅ Certificate issuance
- **Time Commitment**: 8-16 hours (remediation evidence)
- **Cost**: Included (unless major non-conformities require re-audit)

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Corrective action support (if findings exist)
- **Time Commitment**: 10-30 hours
- **Cost**: $1,500-$12,000

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Review of audit reports (especially if findings)
- **Time Commitment**: 3-8 hours
- **Cost**: $900-$6,400

**Customers**:
- ✅ Share audit reports and certificates
- ✅ Update trust centers
- ✅ Customer audit requests may increase

**Regulators**:
- ⚠️ FDA: Premarket approval decision (if submitted)

---

#### **Ongoing: Year 2+**

**External Auditors** (SOC 2 Type II):
- ✅ Continuous observation period evidence collection
- ✅ Annual re-audit
- **Time Commitment**: 40-100 hours annually
- **Cost**: $25,000-$80,000 annually

**Certification Bodies** (ISO):
- ✅ Year 1 Surveillance Audit
- ✅ Year 2 Surveillance Audit
- ✅ Year 3 Recertification Audit (full audit)
- **Time Commitment**: 8-24 hours per surveillance audit
- **Cost**: $3,000-$10,000 per surveillance, $20,000-$60,000 for recertification

**Vendors**:
- ✅ Annual vendor risk assessments
- ✅ Ongoing vendor monitoring
- **Time Commitment**: 20-60 hours annually

**Customers**:
- ✅ Ad-hoc customer audits
- ✅ Security questionnaire responses
- **Time Commitment**: 40-120 hours annually (depends on customer volume)

**Consultants**:
- ✅ Retainer or ad-hoc support
- **Time Commitment**: Variable (0-200 hours annually)
- **Cost**: $0-$80,000 annually

**Legal Counsel**:
- ✅ Ad-hoc questions
- ✅ Annual policy reviews
- **Time Commitment**: 10-40 hours annually
- **Cost**: $3,000-$32,000 annually

**Regulators**:
- ⚠️ FDA: Routine inspections every 2-5 years
- ⚠️ HHS/OCR: Only if breach or complaint
- ⚠️ DPAs: Only if breach or complaint
- ✅ Adverse event reporting (FDA) - ongoing as events occur
- ✅ Breach notifications (HIPAA, GDPR) - as incidents occur

**Insurance**:
- ✅ Annual policy renewal and questionnaire
- **Time Commitment**: 4-12 hours annually

### External Stakeholder Cost Summary

**First Year (Initial Certification/Attestation)**:

| Stakeholder Type | Low Estimate | High Estimate | Notes |
|-----------------|--------------|---------------|-------|
| External Auditors | $15,000 | $80,000 | SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001 |
| Certification Bodies | $20,000 | $60,000 | ISO standards (application through cert) |
| Consultants | $20,000 | $120,000 | Full implementation support |
| Legal Counsel | $10,000 | $50,000 | Policy review, regulatory guidance |
| Vendors/Tools | $10,000 | $100,000+ | Compliance tools, security tools, MSPs |
| **Total First Year** | **$75,000** | **$410,000+** | Highly variable by scope and org size |

**Ongoing Annual Costs** (Years 2+):

| Stakeholder Type | Low Estimate | High Estimate | Notes |
|-----------------|--------------|---------------|-------|
| External Auditors | $25,000 | $80,000 | Annual SOC 2 or ISO surveillance |
| Certification Bodies | $3,000 | $15,000 | ISO surveillance audits |
| Consultants | $0 | $40,000 | Ad-hoc or retainer |
| Legal Counsel | $3,000 | $32,000 | Policy updates, ad-hoc questions |
| Vendors/Tools | $10,000 | $120,000+ | Ongoing subscriptions |
| **Total Annual** | **$41,000** | **$287,000+** | Maintenance mode |

**Additional Costs** (One-Time or Periodic):
- Customer audits: $5,000-$20,000 per audit (internal labor cost)
- Regulatory inspections/responses: $10,000-$100,000+ (depends on findings)
- Major incident response: $50,000-$500,000+ (forensics, legal, PR, remediation)
- Recertification (Year 3): $20,000-$60,000 (full audit cycle)
